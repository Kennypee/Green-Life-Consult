    Acidophilus


    | Overview |

    Intestine-targeted probiotic capsule with 5 billion live micro-organisms for gut health

    | Benefits |

    Regulates intestinal activity. Promotes colon function and supports immunity with a healthy balance of intestinal flora.

    | Details |

    Five billion “live” microorganisms per capsule – as many as in 10 servings of yoghurt, and the convenience of once a day serving. Proprietary blend of five types of clinically proven probiotics: Lactobacillus acidophilus, Lactobacillus bulgaricus, Lactobacillus casei, Bifidobacterium bifidum and Streptococcus thermophilus.
