    Betaguard

    In the modern society it is difficult, perhaps even impossible, to avoid pollution. Car exhaust fumes, cigarette smoke, and factory discharges are all around us. Scientific research shows that certain nutrients may give the body some protection against environmental toxins and help strengthen the body resistance. betaguard is GNLD’s exclusive formula developed to support the body’s natural ability to counteract the effects of toxic pollution.
